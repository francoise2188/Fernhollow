COZY TOWN – PIXEL ART TILESET
16x16 Top-Down Pixel Asset Pack

Thank you for downloading this asset pack!

This pack contains a collection of cozy pixel art assets designed for building small towns, villages, and city environments for 2D games.

---

ASSET DETAILS

Resolution: 16x16 pixels
View: Top-down
Style: Cozy / colorful pixel art

Included content:

• terrain tiles (grass, dirt, cobblestone)
• building parts (walls, roofs, doors, windows)
• platforms and bridges
• decorative props (benches, lamps, signs, fountain, etc.)
• trees and plants
• animated chimney smoke and fountain water
• characters (boy and girl with idle + walk)
• animals (cat and seagull)

Files included:

/tilesets – main tileset sheets
/spritesheets – characters, animals, effects
/individual – separate PNG objects
/preview – example maps and animated previews
/source – Aseprite source files

All graphics are PNG format and should work with most game engines such as Unity, Godot, GameMaker, RPG Maker, and others.
